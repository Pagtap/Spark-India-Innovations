Introduction

SPARK INDIA INNOVATIONS (SII) is a leading company specializing in BMS, panel wiring, SCADA and PLC programming services. Since 2022, we have been delivering reliable, efficient and precision-driven automation solutions for industrial needs.

Our Services

Design

Expert automation system design and engineering consultation.

Manufacturing

Precision panel manufacturing, custom control panel assembly, and fabrication of industrial electrical systems.

Supply & Installation

Supply of automation components and complete on-site installation of panels, cabling, and integrated BMS.

SCADA System Commissioning

End-to-end commissioning of SCADA-integrated electrical systems, including configuration, calibration, and live system validation.
